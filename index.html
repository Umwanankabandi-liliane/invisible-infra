Problem

Infrastructure networks are aging and under-monitored. Many utilities only find issues after service disruptions or expensive repairs. Traditional monitoring requires costly hardware and years of deployment.

Hidden losses

Leakage, degradation, and inefficiencies can persist for months without detection.

Cost of sensors

Full coverage needs large sensor rollouts, maintenance, integration, and skilled teams.

Climate stress

Extreme weather increases failure risk and accelerates wear—prediction matters more than ever.

Solution

Invisible Infra detects anomalies remotely using satellite imagery, environmental signals, and physics-aware ML. It produces risk maps and a prioritized maintenance queue—without requiring new sensors.

What the platform delivers

  • Network risk maps (leaks, losses, degradation hotspots)
  • Prioritized maintenance queue with confidence scores
  • Trend monitoring over time (improving / worsening segments)
  • Explainable drivers (weather, stress, demand patterns)

What you get in weeks

Week 1
Connect available public & customer datasets; set up baseline risk mapping.
Weeks 2–3
Calibrate physics-aware models; validate on historical incidents & work orders.
Weeks 4–6
Deliver actionable hotspots and maintenance prioritization with explainability.
